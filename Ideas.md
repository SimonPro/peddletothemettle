# SKILLS

## Traversal skills

Acrobat. Roll to perform acrobatic feats such as balancing, moving over slippery and precarious terrain, or falling gracefully with Advantage. 

Take this skill again to gain the ability to clamber up even vertical surfaces a close distance up at speed, and to ignore any hindrance posed by obstacles you can vault over or through such as railings and windows. 
_You are encouraged to mutter parkour! parkour! as you roll._

Strong(wo)man. Roll to lift, push, hold and carry heavy things with Advantage, and to crack things open with brute force. The party can carry 5 more supplies. 
_Strong! Strooong!_

Climber. Roll to climb, hold on tight and scramble over terrain with Advantage. 

Swimmer. Roll to swim with Advantage. You can hold your breath for an impressive amount of time. 

Sprinter. Roll to get somewhere fast with Advantage. 

Skulker. Roll to sneak around with Advantage. You make no sound when you move, unless you're in a rush. 

## Social skills

Liar. Roll to blatantly lie or discern you are being lied to with Advantage. 

Animal friend. Attempt to calm down or communicate with animals with Advantage. 

## Knowledge skills

Scholar of ancient languages. 
You can read any language, however ancient and dead. 
You are also roughly understood by elementals, the undead, and other strange and otherwise non-verbal creatures. 
This is sufficient for simple things like _go away_ or _you cannot pass_, but it does not make them any more reasonable or friendly. 
_Back! The ancient tongues summon you back to your grave, creature of the dēop deorc! Loqueris latine! Milás elliniká!_

Reputed scholar. 
Roll to socially interact with scholars, priests, lorekeepers, librarians and similar folk with Advantage. 
_You're Mellas the wizard? Of _Treatise on the winter solstice behavior of juvenile green-striped babosaurs_ fame? I'm a fan!_

Perk Spellbook Lore
When you could Prevent with Magic, you can substitute it with Lore and gain Advantage. 
_She's casting Tashainen's Perfumed Yet Toxic Cloud!_

## Dubious skills

Forgettable. 

Simple. 
Roll to pretend you are lost, confused, unable to comprehend accusations, and to otherwise play dumb with Advantage. 
_Thick is metaphorical! I did not call you fat, I called you an idiot!_

Animal friend/Familiar. Magically bonded to a small animal; see through its eyes, bond to another in downtime. Stays in your vicinity.

# CLASS IDEAS FROM OLDER DESIGN


== Duelist
Just as you attack a foe with a melee weapon, Strain to make it your _dueling foe_. 
In combat, add 1 to rolls related to your dueling foe. 

Perk 
When you overpower a dueling foe, all other lower level foes in the battle become dueling foes. 
Perk
A dueling foe is unable to overpower you. 
Perk
When a hero Prevents an attack from a dueling foe that does not include you but could, Strain to substitute yourself as the target. 
Perk
+1 to Prevent with Nimble, when opposing a dueling foe. 

== Champion
_Paladin, Champion, Templar_

In downtime, you can designate another hero as your protégé. 
You must sit next to them at the table, so pick someone whose company you enjoy, and get their approval first. They can revoke the protégé status anytime. 
Your protégé might be a muse, a romantic interest, or just the bookish and meek wizard you are nevertheless fond of. 

S When you attack with Tough, 
your target is dissuaded to attack your protégé. 
It avoids attacking it, unless also attacking you at the same time, or when it can attack your protégé but not you. 
Struggle ends.

## Guardian
*Armor training.* An armor or heavy armor outfit increases your Damage limit by 2. 

Tortoise Stance
Gain advantage when you Prevent with Tough from an attack that originates from far or further. 
"Arrows filled the sky, but their arc ended harmlessly on her raised shield."

Damage Prevention
S Upon being dealt any amount of damage, Strain to ignore it. 
"Just a flesh wound."
S When you Prevent with Tough, reroll 1s once. 
Spell Mirroring Shield
S When you Prevent, you can substitute Magic with Tough. You must wield a shield. 
"Lightning crashed across his shield, sending sparks flying around him."
Shield Assist
S When a hero close to you would Prevent, Strain to interpose yourself and Prevent in their stead. If you would also Prevent the same thing regardless, do it only once for yourself. 
_Running with the grace of a speedy armored tortoise, he interposed his shield to safeguard the meek._

## Seer

Strain to open your third eye for a short time. 
When you third eye is open, you see through obscurity, foliage, rain, fog, curtains, and so on. Opaque objects still block your sight. 

You can also take a short time to draw or carve eye shapes on things around you, like a stone or tree. The game master will tell you if something interesting happens to pass the eye. As an Action before you leave its vicinity, you can see through an eye as though located there for a moment. 

Nudge Fate
Whenever you roll, Strain to turn one die to the next higher number. 


## Druid
This class may contain nuts. 
Seriously, if there are nut allergies at your table, substitute them with candy or play something else. 

Procure a bag of peanuts, cashews or other crunchy delight. 
If you can easily procure them each session, berries are also acceptable. 

You dispense blessings: metaphorical (advice) or physical (magical berries). 
Your nuts also count as threat tokens.
